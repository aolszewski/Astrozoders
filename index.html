What does the Sun Sign mean?

Your Sun Sign is the sign of the Zodiac where the Sun was situated at the moment of a person's birth. It is associated with the essential qualities of one's character and with the pupose for which you are born. .

What is a Horoscope?

Horoscope is a forecast or predication of your future based on the influece of celestial bodies such as the sun, moon and the planets.

What is a Tarot reading?

Tarot card reading is the practice of using tarot cards to gain insight into the past, present or future by formulating a question, then drawing and interpreting cards. Reading tarot cards is a type of cartomancy.
